I can't wait to share everything I've learned with you so you too can become a great game developer in no time. Hi My name is Sergey, I am an economic game designer at Ubisoft Montreal. Ever since I was young, I've always been interested in creating games; whether it was inventing RPGs with my sister, creating huge Lego worlds with story adventures or creating Excel spreadsheets for Diablo. From childhood, my path was always clear - I would work in the video game industry one way or another. As an adult, I continued that path and earned two degrees; one in marketing and the other in game and level design. During my studies I delved into many theoretical topics about game design and development such as; Freemium Business Model, gamification, KPI analysis for optimization, level design, and color psychology to name a few. There has been a lot of trial and error in my personal approaches, which are mistakes I want others to avoid, so I started creating these courses to help developers unfamiliar with the industry not make the same mistakes that I, and many others, have made.
