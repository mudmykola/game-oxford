Who is suitable for this course

  • People with no experience in IT

    You'll get basic programming, analytics, statistics, and mathematics skills that will open the way to a career in Data Science and Machine Learning. You'll be able to use your knowledge immediately in practice.

  • Programmers

    You will pump up your knowledge and skills in Python and R programming. You'll improve your math and analytical thinking skills, use machine learning algorithms to solve business problems - and strengthen your portfolio with powerful projects.

  • For novice analysts

    You will learn how to make hypotheses and draw conclusions based on data, write efficient code in Python and R, turn raw data into useful information for the company, understand mathematics based on statistics, as well as to train machines and predict results. You'll polish your knowledge, increase the speed of your work, and get promoted.
