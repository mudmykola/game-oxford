The objective price for the course is 5,000 hryvnia. After completing the course, you'll get the full price back in a month as a trainee. However, our goal is not a large average check, but a large number of satisfied clients and the consistency of orders.

  • Low advertising costs

    We do not aim to sell. Our main goal is to provide courses that are accessible and understandable to absolutely everyone.

  • Many loyal customers

    We have many subscribers and clients, with at least several hundred orders for each course among our audience.

  • No intermediaries

    The author of the course is a teacher himself and does not need a large number of intermediaries and hired teachers.
